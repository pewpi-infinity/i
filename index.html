Octave OS v0.12 — Secret Encoder (Pewpi)
Type a message, press "Create secret" — it gives you an encoded blob and a passphrase to share securely.

Quick: create a secret from your message

How it works: the page derives a strong key from the passphrase (PBKDF2) and encrypts your message with AES‑GCM. Output is a single base64 string containing salt+iv+ciphertext. Recipient needs the base64 string + the passphrase to recover the message.

Decode a secret

Notes: Everything runs in your browser. Do not paste raw secrets into pages you don't control. Share the encoded string and passphrase only with the intended recipient.
